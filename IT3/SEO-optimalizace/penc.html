What is nature?

Nature refers to the natural world around us, including plants, animals, landscapes, and ecosystems.

Why is nature important?

Nature provides us with many benefits, such as clean air and water, food and medicine, and recreational opportunities. It is also home to a vast array of species that contribute to the biodiversity of our planet.

Explore nature

There are many ways to explore and enjoy nature, including hiking, camping, birdwatching, and more.
